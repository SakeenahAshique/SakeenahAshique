Meet me as an Artist:

I believe there are many lenses in which I may introduce myself to an individual, one of my many sides is my creativity, hence this website. I am most passionate about oil painting. Oil painting is by far my favorite medium. It has this ability to be harsh and brilliant, yet soft. I have yet to find a different medium that I believe has as much depth as oil paints. My artist inspiration comes from personal experiences that have been shaped by my unique upbringing, heavily inspired by my experiences, religious beliefs, and a hint of my own femininity. I believe in thinking outside the box and allowing people to interpret art the way they wish, which is why I primarily pursue abstract art.
